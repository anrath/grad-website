I am graduating!

The dates are 17th through 19th of May '24 and I invite you to be a part of it.

My Journey at UVA

I started my journey at UVA in the Fall of 2020, during COVID-19. I completed my first year online and moved to Charlottesville in the Fall of 2021. I graduated with Bachelors' degrees in Computer Science and Financial Economics in 2023. This year, I will be completing my Master's in Computer Science.

Procession Down the Lawn9AM-11AM

Ugh... travel?

Hotel

There are many options in Charlottesville but graduation season is very busy. I will send a list of options to those who RSVP. I suggest calling the hotel directly to book as soon as you get the list. They fill up quickly.

Flights

You probably should not be booking flights for some time. CHO is the aiport of choice. If you would like to sight-see in Washington, DC before coming to my graduation, you can fly to IAD or DCA. The drive from DC to Charlottesville is about 2.5 hours.
